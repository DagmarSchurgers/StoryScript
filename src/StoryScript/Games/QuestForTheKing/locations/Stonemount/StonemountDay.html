You come upon a small mount of stones. The stones have been placed too orderly for it to be a natural formation. It seems to have been made a long time ago, and is partially overgrown with grass and ferns.

As you come closer, you suddenly shiver with a strange chill, as if someone touched you with icy fingers. But the feeling quickly fades away again.

Suddenly, from the corners of your eyes, you spot a swift shadow moving between the trees. Another appears between the trees on your other side. You hear a sudden growling sound behind you. As you turn, you are faced with two large wolfs, emerging from the forest and advancing towards you, growling and with bared teeth.

The two wolves lie defeated before you. Their appetite for adventurers has proved too much for them.’
