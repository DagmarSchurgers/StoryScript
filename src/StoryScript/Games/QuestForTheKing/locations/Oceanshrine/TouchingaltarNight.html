You reach out your hands and touch the altar. As you do so, you feel a feeling of peace and calm flow through you. You feel as if you are in touch with everything around you. The stars in the night sky, the gentle ocean waves, the tingling music of the wind. You feel as if it is all part of you.

For a moment, you feel almost weightless, as if all the weight and worries of the world have been lifted from your shoulders.

You are fully healed.

The Wizard

You feel the presence of magic. Benevolent magic, coming from the altar. But you detect another presence as well. Just before the altar, beneath your feet. You take a step back, and observe that one of the marble stones before the altar seems loose.

As you remove the stone, you see a small cavity, and within it, a small leather bag. You open the bag, and find a silver ring, adorned with a large ruby. The ring glows up in your hands with a fiery light.
