With a groan of pain, Brennus falls heavily to the ground. After some moments, he manages to raise himself a bit, resting on the pommel of his sword.

‘Alas, you have defeated me. Well, you did good, and I suppose you are the better warriors. I leave it to you then, to complete the quest of the king.’ Brennus raises his hand in a salute. ‘Off with you lads, your adventure awaits.’
