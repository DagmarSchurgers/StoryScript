As you hold out the magic flower to the girl, her eyes widen. Slowly, she begins to swims closer, but very cautiously, watching you intently. The flower, meanwhile, starts to glow with a misty blue light.

The girl reaches for the flower with a slender arm, and takes the magic flower in her hand. She retreats back into the sea. Then, she dives beneath the water with a strong motion of her tail.

A large clamshell floats towards the beach. It is taken up by the surf and pushed unto the sand. It opens out of itself, revealing a large, shiny pearl. A soft whisper seems to reach your ears, embedded within the rustling of the ocean surf. ‘Thank you for the beautiful flower’.
