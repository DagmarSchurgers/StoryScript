You enter a forest clearing. A colourful wagon stands in the centre of the clearing. In front of the wagon, surrounded by a circle of stones, burns a small campfire. Near the fire, a figure sits huddled in a dark cloak.

As you approach the figure raises himself from the ground. You see a middle-aged man with dark skin and black eyes. He has a black moustache and short black curles. ‘Welcome, adventures.’ The man bows gracefully before you. ‘You are in need of something, yes? I can see it in your eyes.’ The man smiles, bearing a number of gold teeth.

‘And whatever you need, friends, I can provide. Weapons and armour. Swords and bows of the highest quality. And magic items. Strange, mysterious items of such power as to be found nowhere else. Oh, and I can teach you a skill or two, if you are of a certain profession.’ The man grins, and awaits your choice.
