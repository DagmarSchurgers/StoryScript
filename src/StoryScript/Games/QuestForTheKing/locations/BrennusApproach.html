As you approach the tent, you can hear some faint voices coming from within. Suddenly, some dried twigs crack beneath your boots. Within the tent the voices immediately stop.

‘Wait’ a deep male voice exclaims. ‘Did you hear something?’

You hear heavy footsteps approaching the flap of the tent. The next moment, the tent flap is cast aside. A large warrior stands in the opening, clad in heavy plate mail. He wears a black beard and moustache, a large scar runs down his left cheek.

‘What’s this? Assassins come to murder me in the night? I’ll kill you for your dishonour.’ The warrior draws a large sword from a scabbard at his side.
