With a groan of pain, Brennus falls heavily to the ground. After some moments, he manages to raise himself a bit, resting on the pommel of his sword. ‘And so the mighty Brennus is defeated. By thugs and mercenaries in the bleak of the night. Well, have at it then, end the days of the mighty Brennus.’

As you stand back, and allow Brennus to rise slowly and painfully to his feet, and even offer him the support of your arm, he eyes you with renewed interest.

‘So, not likely to be criminals, you are. Perhaps I misjudged you. Perhaps you are good folk. Well, defeated me you have, and as you are fellow adventures, from the looks of it, you too are probably on the quest for the king. You have bested me, so it is all yours.’ Brennus raises his hand in a salute. ‘Now off with you lads, your adventure awaits.
