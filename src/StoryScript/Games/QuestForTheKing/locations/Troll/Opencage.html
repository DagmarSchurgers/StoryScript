As you open the iron cage, the small butterfly-like creatures immediately dart to freedom. As they swoop past you, you can see that they resemble tiny elves or faeries, glistening in the sunlight with a silvery hue. One of the faeries stops in mid-flight, and hovers before you.

‘Thank you for saving us’ it seems to say, in a barely audible, tinkling voice. Then it swiftly flies away after the others.
